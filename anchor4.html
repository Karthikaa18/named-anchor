HISTORY OF COMPUTERS

THIRD GENERATION COMPUTERS(1964 to 1971)

  1. Although transistors were great deal of improvement over the vacuum tubes, they generated heat and damaged the sensitive areas of the computer.
  2. The Intergreated Circuit(IC) was invented in 1958 by Jack Kilby. It combined electronic components onto a small silicon disc, made from quartz.
  3. More advancement made possible the fitings of even more components on a small chip or a semi conductor.
  4. Also in third generation computers, the operating systems allowed the machines to run many different applications.
  5. These applications were monitored and coordinated by the computer's memory.
